Achievements

Achievement Description
Freebie Sign into slack and post a team name in the #gamedevduelcheevos channel (https://stlgamedev.slack.com/messages/gamedevduelcheevos)
Movement Make something happen when you interact with the game
Artsy Add at least one art asset into the game
Slacker Create a private slack channel and invite your team to coordinate game progress
Tracker Set up a Kanban board (https://trello.com/ or the tool of your choice) to prioritize tasks and track progress
Check-in #1 Meet up with your team in person. We recommend the check-in event at Riot Games on July 6th (featuring Butterscotch Shenanigans)
Git it on If your game isn't in version control by now, add it to version control (github.com, bitbucket.com, Subversion)
Hardcore #1 Everyone on the team worked on the game for over 3 hours in one sitting before July 8th
Weekender #1 At least one person on the team spent at least 8 hours over the weekend of July 1st to July 3rd working on the game
Game Loop You have the core gameplay loop working in your game
Named Finalize the name of your game so that you can begin marketing it
StandUp Post a Daily Standup in the Slack channel. In a Standup, mention what you did yesterday, and what you plan to do today. Standups are a great way to keep you focused on your goals!
Screenshot Saturday #1 July 9th Screenshot Saturday! Post a screenshot to Twitter with the name of your game and the hashtags #screenshotsaturday and #gamedevduel and/or the Game Dev Co-op Facebook page (http://facebook.com/stlgamedev) with the hashtag #screenshotsaturday
Publisher Build an itch.io page for your game under our Game Jam
Songster Have at least one song in your game
Check-in #2 Meet up with your team in person to evaluate progress and discuss plans. If your whole team is over 21, meet up at the check-in on July 14th at Orbit Pinball Lounge.
Death Your game has a loss state (free achievement if this is not applicable)
Winner Your game has a level or sub-section complete state working (free achievement if this is not applicable)
Hardcore #2 Everyone on the team worked on the game for over 3 hours in one sitting between July 8th and July 15th
Weekender #2 At least one person on the team spent at least 8 hours over the weekend of July 8th to July 10th working on the game
Menus Add a title screen to your game (free achievement if this is not applicable)
Screenshot Saturday #2 July 16 Post a screenshot to Twitter with the name of your game and the hashtags #screenshotsaturday and #gamedevduel and/or the Game Dev Co-op Facebook page (http://facebook.com/stlgamedev) with the hashtag #screenshotsaturday
Face Off Add at least one sound effect to the game that was recorded by a team member and uses either the environment or a face
Feedback Have someone play your game
Check-in #3 Meet up with your team in person. We recommend the check-in event at Riot Games on July 20th
The Chopping Block Talk to the team and cut at least two planned features and/or assets from your game
Diverse If your game has more than one character in it, make sure at least one of them is not a straight white person (free achievement if this is not applicable)
Archie Put a reference to or asset of the St. Louis arch somewhere in your game
Hardcore #3 Everyone on the team worked on the game for over 3 hours in one sitting between July 15th and July 22nd
Weekender #3 At least one person on the team spent at least 8 hours over the weekend of July 15th to July 17th working on the game
Check-in #4 Meet up with your team in person. We recommend the check-in event the last week of July
Screenshot Saturday #3 July 23rd Post a screenshot to Twitter with the name of your game and the hashtags #screenshotsaturday and #gamedevduel and/or the Game Dev Co-op Facebook page (http://facebook.com/stlgamedev) with the hashtag #screenshotsaturday
Screenshot Saturday #4 July 30th Post a screenshot to Twitter with the name of your game and the hashtags #screenshotsaturday and #gamedevduel and/or the Game Dev Co-op Facebook page (http://facebook.com/stlgamedev) with the hashtag #screenshotsaturday
Finish Line Finish your game!
Control for All Your game supports both mouse and keyboard and controller input methods
Credits Add a credits screen to your game
Made in St Louis Add the Made in St. Louis Splash Screen to your game
Hardcore #4 Everyone on the team worked on the game for over 3 hours in one sitting between July 22nd and July 29th
Weekender #4 At least one person on the team spent at least 8 hours over the weekend of July 22nd to July 25th working on the game
Weekender #5 At least one person on the team spent at least 8 hours over the weekend of July 29th to July 31st working on the game
Buddy-Up Your game features multiplayer gameplay (local or online)
... Your game contains no written or spoken words, not even instructions (but should be intuitive!)
Pacifism Your game contains no violence or gore
Public Domain Your game's story, character(s), and/or theme are based on works in the public domain
Magically Delicious Your game contains elements of magic, witchcraft, and/or wizardry
Scientifically Accurate Your game contains no fantastical elements; only things that exist in the 'real world'
Historical Your game is based on an actual historic event - even if only loosely
One Screen Your game takes place entirely on a single screen (Pac-man, Donkey Kong, etc)
Written Word Your game relies heavily on written word, dialog, story (interactive fiction, RPGs, etc)
