Achievements

Achievement Description Phase
Freebie Sign into slack and post a team name in the #gamedevduelcheevos channel (https://stlgamedev.slack.com/messages/gamedevduelcheevos) First Week
Movement Make something happen when you interact with the game First Week
Artsy Add at least one art asset into the game First Week
Slacker Create a private slack channel and invite your team to coordinate game progress First Week
Tracker Set up a Kanban board (https://trello.com/ or the tool of your choice) to prioritize tasks and track progress First Week
Check-in #1 Meet up with your team in person. We recommend the check-in event at Riot Games on July 6th (featuring Butterscotch Shenanigans) First Week
Git it on If your game isn't in version control by now, add it to version control (github.com, bitbucket.com, Subversion) First Week
Hardcore #1 Everyone on the team worked on the game for over 3 hours in one sitting before July 8th First Week
Weekender #1 At least one person on the team spent at least 8 hours over the weekend of July 1st to July 3rd working on the game First Week
Game Loop You have the core gameplay loop working in your game First Week
Diverse If your game has characters in it, make sure at least one of them is not a straight white person (free if your game has no characters) Ongoing
Open Source Your game is open source and the code is available in a public source control repository such as github.com or bitbucket.com Ongoing
The Internet Your game contains a picture, gif, or video of a cat Ongoing
Committed Someone on your team has made at least one commit to source control on every day in July Ongoing
Multiple Interpretations Your game unquestionably demonstrates more than one interperitation of the Game Jam theme Ongoing
Accessible Your game can be played effectively by someone who is either blind or has no limbs Ongoing
Character Development A main character in your game changes over the course of the game (free if your game has no characters) Ongoing
Shared Assets Your game features an asset or character from some other gamedevduel team. (coordinate through Slack) Ongoing
Orchestrated Your game features at least three songs that were written specifically for your game Ongoing
Archie Put a reference to or asset of the St. Louis arch somewhere in your game Ongoing
